RNA Reconstruction

Instructions:

Please input the RNA fragments in each textbox, separated by spaces. Use the following format:

For G-enzyme fragments: AUCG AUG G CU ACUAUACG

For U.C-enzyme fragments: GGAC U AU GAU C U AC GC AU

Sample Input:

G-enzyme: AUCG AUG G CU ACUAUACG

U.C-enzyme: GGAC U AU GAU C U AC GC AU
